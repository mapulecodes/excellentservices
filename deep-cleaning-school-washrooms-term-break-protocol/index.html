Deep Cleaning School Washrooms: A Term Break Protocol for Western Cape Schools

Step-by-step washroom deep cleaning checklist and recommended scheduling for primary and high schools preparing facilities for the next academic term.

Term break provides schools with the ideal window for washroom deep cleaning — facilities are unoccupied, deep sanitisation treatments can be applied without disruption, and grout, tile and cistern cleaning can be completed properly.

A recommended term break protocol covers: full tile and grout scrub with commercial-grade sanitiser, cistern disinfection, drain clearing and deodorising, sanitary bin deep clean and liner replacement, mirror and fixture restoration, and a final disinfection treatment of all touch-point surfaces (taps, door handles, flushers).

Schools that document this process as part of their health and safety records support good hygiene management and reduce the risk of washroom-related illness transmission at the start of each term.

Excellent Services schedules school deep cleans during Western Cape term breaks — contact us in advance to secure a booking for your school’s washrooms.
